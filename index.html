Microsoft Patents Ones and Zeroes

Unexpected development shakes up computer industry

REMOND,WA: In what CEO Bill Gates called an unfortunate but necesarry step to protect our intelectual property from theft ans exploitation by competitors, the Microsoft Corporation patented the numbers one and zero Monday.

With the patent ,Microsoft's rivals are prohibited from manufacturing or selling products containing zeroes and ones unless a royalty fee of 10 cents per digit used is paid to the software giant.

Some other patents that Microsoft owns:

  • Windows Vista
  • Microsoft Office
  • Xbox 360
